FINANCE

OVERVIEW

What is Finance?

Finance is the study and management of money, investments, banking, and risk management. It focuses on how individuals, businesses, and governments allocate financial resources efficiently. In Australia, the finance sector is one of the highest-paying and most competitive industries, with opportunities in banking, investment, financial planning, and corporate finance.

What Will You Do?

  • Analyzing financial markets and investment opportunities.
  • Managing budgets, financial statements, and profitability.
  • Advising individuals and businesses on financial planning and risk management.
  • Handling mergers, acquisitions, and capital allocation.
  • Ensuring companies comply with financial regulations in Australia.

Salary Range in Australia

Entry-Level (0-2 years): AU$65,000 - AU$85,000 per year
Mid-Level (3-5 years): AU$90,000 - AU$140,000 per year
Senior-Level (5+ years): AU$150,000 - AU$300,000+ per year
Top executives & investment banking: AU$500,000+ per year

2. Steps to Enter the Finance Industry in Australia

Step 1: Get a Finance Education

  • Study economics, financial modeling, investment management.
  • Learn how banking systems, stock markets, and corporate finance work.

Step 2: Gain Practical Experience

  • Apply for internships in banks, investment firms, and corporate finance.
  • Participate in finance case competitions (CFA Research Challenge, FMAA competitions).

Step 3: Gain Professional Certifications

‚úÖ Recommended Certifications

  • CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) ‚Äì for investment and portfolio management.
  • CPA (Certified Practising Accountant) ‚Äì for financial management and accounting.
  • FRM (Financial Risk Manager) ‚Äì for risk management roles.

Step 4: Build a Strong Network

  • Join finance societies (FMAA, CFA Institute, CPA Australia).
  • Connect with finance professionals and recruiters on LinkedIn.

Step 5: Apply for Graduate Finance Roles

  • Target firms in investment banking, asset management, consulting.
  • Look for roles in banks (ANZ, NAB, Commonwealth Bank), consulting firms (EY, Deloitte), and fund management companies.

MARKET ANALYSIS & TREND PREDICTION

Current Market Snapshot

Current market size: Approximately $398.7 billion globally (2024).

Top 3 cities/regions with highest demand:

New York City, USA

Global financial capital with Wall Street, major investment banks, hedge funds, and financial services firms. High demand for finance professionals across investment banking, asset management, private equity, and fintech. Houses headquarters of major financial institutions and regulatory bodies.

London, UK

Europe's financial hub with a strong presence in investment banking, insurance, and international finance. Home to the London Stock Exchange and European headquarters of global financial institutions. High concentration of wealth management and financial advisory services.

Hong Kong/Singapore

Asia's dual financial centers with strong demand in banking, wealth management, and capital markets. Strategic locations for financial institutions seeking presence in Asian markets. Growing fintech ecosystems creating new finance career opportunities.
